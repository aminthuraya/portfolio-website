ABOUT

My Story

Hello and welcome… I am a UI designer living in the Greater Toronto Area (GTA), in the beautiful town of Oakville with it’s picturesque harbours. a creative eye and a passion for beautiful interfaces. My background in graphic design, HR and tutoring evolves around people. As a graphic designer I work with clients to translate their ideas or business challenges into design that is structured and well thought out, I do this by listening to my clients, looking at the full picture from all perspectives and translating the idea into a coherent visual story.

Being able to solve problems with solid solutions derived from research, communication, and iterations are only some of the aspects that drew me to UI/UX design. CareerFoundry’s UI Design course advanced my knowledge of design methodologies, wireframing, usability testing and additional tools like Sketch and InVision. I truly enjoy excelling and pushing myself.

I am also a CareerFoundry tutor, which I very much enjoy. Supporting and guiding my students throughout their UI design journey, personalizing and humanizing the process is my approach.

When I am not designing or tutoring, I love to cook and have the freedom to create my own recipes. I will travel whenever I get the opportunity especially to Europe exploring cities and nature. It doesn’t take much make me happy, a meal, a card game or just a casual chat with my boys and husband will do the trick!
